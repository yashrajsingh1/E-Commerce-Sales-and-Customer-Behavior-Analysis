# Business Insights & Recommendations

## 1. Revenue & Growth

| Insight | Evidence | Recommendation |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Revenue peaks in Nov–Dec (Q4) | Monthly trend chart | Run email campaigns from late October; pre-stock top SKUs |
| Slow Q1 and Q3 | Quarterly chart | Introduce mid-year promotion to flatten seasonality |
| Revenue grew ~25% YoY (2010→2011) | YoY comparison query | Invest in geographic expansion in fastest-growing markets |
| Thursday is the top day; Sunday the lowest | Day-of-week chart | Schedule promotions on Wed/Thu evenings |
| Peak hours: 10 AM – 12 PM | Hourly chart | Time email sends for 9:30–10:00 AM |

## 2. Geography

| Insight | Evidence | Recommendation |

## 3. Products

| Insight | Evidence | Recommendation |

## 4. Customer Segmentation (RFM)

| Segment | % of Customers | % of Revenue | Action |
|---------|---------------|-------------|--------|
| Champions | ~8% | ~38% | VIP rewards, early access, referral scheme |
| Loyal Customers | ~15% | ~28% | Upsell premium lines, loyalty points |
| Potential Loyalists | ~18% | ~14% | 2nd-purchase incentive email |
| At Risk | ~12% | ~11% | Win-back campaign: "We miss you" + 15% discount |
| Need Attention | ~10% | ~6% | Personalised recommendations |
| Recent Customers | ~9% | ~4% | Onboarding series (3 emails, 30 days) |
| Promising | ~11% | ~3% | First loyalty reward |
| Lost | ~17% | ~2% | Low-cost reactivation or remove from list |

---

## 5. Retention Finding

- **Repeat buyer rate**: approximately 37% of customers place more than one order
- **Implication**: 63% are one-time buyers — huge retention opportunity
- **Action**: introduce a post-purchase "thank you + 10% off next order" automation

---

## 6. Operational Recommendations

1. **Inventory**: Align stock of top 10 products to Q4 demand (+40% buffer)
2. **Pricing**: Test 5% price increase on Champions' favourite SKUs (low elasticity expected)
3. **Marketing budget shift**: Reallocate 20% of UK ad spend to Germany/France
4. **CRM automation**: Build 5 triggered email flows (welcome, post-purchase, win-back, VIP, lapsed)
5. **Dashboard**: Share Power BI report with marketing and ops teams weekly

---

## 7. Metrics to Track Going Forward (North Star KPIs)

- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) — survey Champions and At-Risk groups
- Churn rate month-over-month
